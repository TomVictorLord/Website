1.5 Describe how you would go about selecting and using IT systems and software

Research each system and software and see which you find the easiest to use and believe would be the most cost efficient and effective, eg, different game engines are better for a certain genre of game, eg,2-D top-down shooters, but may not be the best or even possible with others, eg,3-D Open world.
Research what the software has created in the past and see how it compares with what you intend to create and how successful it was.
Compare different software and technologies functionality, features and cost to what’s already out on the market and what will be out in the future.
Look up specs such as when buying a computer, things like RAM, storage and graphics card to see exactly what you're paying for to ensure no money is wasted on features you won’t need for the project.
