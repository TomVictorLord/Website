3.1 Review the benefits and drawbacks of IT tools and systems used in terms of productivity and efficiency

The benefits of IT tools is that they are usable anywhere with an internet connection and your work is accessible from home.
There is also plenty of information to get learning each program on the internet although some can be a bit daunting to learn, like Visual Studio for people not familiar with computers or new to coding.
But the effort is worth the pay off as lots of sites are free and well made.
Working in teams would have been possible over sites like slack where you can upload your work to the cloud and easily send code between people.
Someone who's good at HTML could do that side and another could do the CSS to save time.
